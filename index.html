about

Merrin is web developer with a background in operations, human rights and customer service. She has spent her career connecting people to their communities and the things they love, whether through computers, sports, food or parks. She hopes her work as a developer will continue to expand on this passion for connection, community and joy. She has a minor in Computer Science from the University of Delaware and has spent years learning, improving and growing her programming skills through bootcamps and online courses. She also has a BA in Political Science and Masters in International Human Rights from the London School of Economics.

Professionally, Merrin has spent her time organizing, problem solving and leading. She laid the ground work for programs that connected communities to the outdoors and public lands. She spent 12 years as an Operations Manager and group leader at a summer camp in the Adirondacks and 3 years a Program Manager for the National Park Service, where she managed volunteer operations and community engagement programs that helped bring the city of Boston closer to its green spaces. She has also worked to resettle refugees in Philadelphia and Salt Lake City and as a bartender, where she worked on amazing teams with people from all over the world.

She is excited about her current chapter as a web developer and cannot wait to work with you to bring the things you are passionate about to life online.
